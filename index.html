About me

I am a fourth year PhD student in the MIGSAA CDT, based at the University of Edinburgh and working under the supervision of Jonas Azzam.

My main area of interest is geometric measure theory. More specifically, I'm interested in quantitative aspects of rectifiability, both in Euclidean and non-Euclidean settings.

I hold a Masters of Mathematics (MMATH) from Durham University.
