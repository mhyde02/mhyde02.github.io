About me

I am an Academy Research Fellow based at the University of Jyväskylä (Finland). Previously, I was a postdoc at the University of Warwick, working with David Bate. I obtained my PhD in 2021 at the University of Edinburgh, under the supervision of Jonas Azzam.

My main area of interest is geometric measure theory. More specifically, I'm interested in quantitative aspects of rectifiability and differentiability in both Euclidean and non-Euclidean settings.
